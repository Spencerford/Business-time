About BachHealth

BachHealth summits spotlight emerging healthcare solutions and innovators that provide transparency, lower costs, improve accessibility and overall healthier outcomes and connect those solutions with the population they serve.

What We Do

We see technology adoption and emerging models of healthcare delivery as the catalyst for lower costs and better outcomes for patients and providers challenged with today’s inefficient healthcare system. BachHealth technology integrations and regional healthcare summits power a large-scale movement for providers and buyers of healthcare services to identify emerging solutions and partner to encourage a local healthcare eco-system that is competitive, agile and more effective.






BachHealth Summits Support:

Employers and their channel partners, brokers/TPAs/consultants/etc, healthcare providers and systems, insurers and innovators to develop collaborations. These partnerships identify and implement best-practice solutions that provide new models of healthcare delivery and access in regional markets.
